Founder’s Narrative: The Architect of Conscious Transformation

"Abdullah Yusuf (Abu Hamza): The Somali-American founder who reconciled global success sciences with their spiritual roots, helping you live a life of wholeness, abundance, and unfragmented power."

Nasiiba Coaching® is the culmination of a 25-year transformative journey led by Abdullah Yusuf (Abu Hamza)—a visionary Somali American awareness and development coach with deep-rooted expertise in education, training, and social entrepreneurship.

While inspired by the global pioneers of personal success—Andrew Carnegie, Napoleon Hill, Earl Nightingale, and Bob Proctor—Abu Hamza has pioneered a unique evolution. He has successfully reconnected this intellectual heritage with its Divine source, stripping away pure materialism to offer a holistic model. By integrating authentic spiritual wisdom with modern coaching tools mastered through his professional journey in the United States, he empowers individuals to align with their Creator, their true selves, and their life’s mission.

Born from the fundamental question, "How can one live at full capacity… without being fragmented?"—Nasiiba is the definitive answer. It is a gateway to comprehensive awareness and real-world transformation.

What Makes Us Unique (Our 5 Pillars)

  • Spiritual-Professional Synthesis: We seamlessly integrate deep spiritual awareness with global professional excellence.
  • Transformation Over Motivation: Our methodology focuses on permanent, awareness-based shifts rather than fleeting emotional highs.
  • Sustainable Human & Corporate Impact: We deliver measurable coaching results that create a lasting legacy for individuals and organizations.
  • Multilingual & Global Reach: Our programs are delivered in multiple languages to empower diverse communities across borders.
  • Humanity-First Mission: We are a mission-driven organization where human-centered values precede commercial interests.
